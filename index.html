WORK

Portfolio of Projects

A very brief collection of some of the work that I've been a part of in the last few years, including classwork, professional work, and passion projects.

Ball State Chirper App

React Native / Android + iOS

The BSU Chirper App is the official sports app of the University, which currently has around 2000 downloads.The app was released on the Google Play Store as well as on the iOS App store, though the team only created one file (the power of React Native!) I was actively involved in the creation, bug fixes, and maintenance of this project.

Innovate WithIN

ReactJS / PHP

Innovate WithIN is a state-wide middle and high school competition which teaches Hoosiers to engage in entrepreneurship and idea generation, with a grand prize of ten-thousand dollars for the winner. The website took nearly a year to design, engineer, and build, and I helped create the backend, database, and front-facing user site.

WordPress Multisite

WordPress / PHP

BSU Multisite was one of the largest undertakings of the Digital Corps, my college employer, in which we built 2 custom WP themes, 3 custom WP plugins, and a network of WP sites to be created and used internally at Ball State University by professors, faculty, and departments, and even a few local organizations and companies.

BSU Sales and Marketing

HMTL / CSS

Sales and Marketing, more formally titled the BSU Center for Professional Selling, is a website built with with WordPress and React, but my primary role was to help create the HTML skeleton and learn CSS/SCSS styling. I also helped create carousel functionality and custom WP taxonomy fields for users, to help with the overall experience.

Innovative Educator Conference

MailChimp / PHP

The IEC is an educational conference hosted by the creators of Innovate WithIN, a project listed above, for teachers and entrepreneurs to come and hone their skills amongst others like them from Indiana. My role on the project was to create a registration system through MailChimp and CURL, as well as an email 'campaign'.

OneAmerica Insurance

SQL / ReactJS

My senior year at Ball State included a two semester long project where myself and a team of other students worked with OneAmerica, an Indianapolis based company. We worked to expand their current website through the addition of an insurance calculator which could be used by a user, and accessed through an employee database we created.
